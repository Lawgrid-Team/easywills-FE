Privacy Policy

Table of contents

1. Introduction

easyWills is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our platform. By using easyWills, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and contact details.
  • Estate and Will Related Information: Details about assets, beneficiaries, executors, and any other information inputted into the will writing wizard.
  • Financial Information: Payment details (processed securely by third party payment providers).
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type, and interaction history with our platform.
  • Video and Electronic Signature Data: Video recordings of will execution sessions and digital signature logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate creation, execution, and secure storage of your Will.
  • Verify user identity and ensure legal compliance.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve platform security and user experience.
  • Process transactions and manage billing.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

4. Data storage and security

We implement strict security measures to protect user data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information.
  • Secure cloud based storage for digital wills and execution records.
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

Your data is stored for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Upon request, we can delete your personal data, except where retention is legally required.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal data. However, we may share information:

  • With authorized legal professionals for consultation (only with user consent).
  • With third party service providers for payment processing, authentication, and data storage.
  • If required by law or in response to legal proceedings.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access and review personal data collected by easyWills.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (this may limit service functionality).
  • Request deletion of personal data, subject to legal limitations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

8. Compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)

We comply with the NDPR and adhere to its principles of:

  • Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency in data processing.
  • Data minimization and accuracy.
  • Security and confidentiality of personal information.

9. Contact Information

For privacy related inquiries or data access requests, contact us at admin@lawgrid.ng
